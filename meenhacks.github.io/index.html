About Keep Austin Hacking

On September 4th, in Austin, we are hosting Central Texas's largest hackathon student led: Keep Austin Hacking, where 300 of Central Texas's brightest student hackers across 6 grade levels will compete, learn, and innovate.

Over the course of 24 hours, small teams composed of student attendees will develop their own apps, websites, and games. In addition, students can compete in a competitve programing contest, interact with mentors, listen to keynote speakers, and participate in workshops. After time is up a panel of judges will choose the best projects for prizes and demos. Although many attendees will be new to coding, through this collaborative learning experience they will transform from passive consumers of technology to avid creators.

We are committed to ensuring that students of all demographic backgrounds can attend. We encourage the participation of all genders, underrepresented minorities, and LGBTQ+ students.

Participants we recomend bringing a laptop, charger, pillow and blanket, and a fun positive attitude!

CPC

CPC stands for Competitive Programing Contest. Our CPC problems and inline IDE and autograder will be available only during competion day. Our problems will be similiar to USACO and other competitve programing problems.
